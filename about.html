About Us

MSES LTD (MEP Service and Equipment Solutions) is a forward-looking engineering company based in Kigali, Rwanda, dedicated to providing eco-efficient mechanical and electrical engineering solutions. We specialize in delivering sustainable, high-quality services to residential, commercial, and industrial clients, ensuring that every project meets modern standards of energy efficiency, safety, and comfort. Guided by our motto, “Eco-efficient Mechanical & Electrical Engineering”, we aim to shape the future of the MEP industry by integrating green technologies, smart automation, and preventive maintenance strategies into all our projects. Our unique dual expertise in both mechanical and electrical systems allows us to deliver faster, smarter, and more cost-effective solutions with minimal reliance on subcontractors. At MSES LTD, our team of certified professionals combines technical excellence with a deep understanding of each client’s specific requirements. This enables us to design and implement solutions that are not only reliable and efficient but also sustainable and future-ready.

Vision

Our vision is to become a regional leader in eco-efficient mechanical and electrical engineering, recognized for sustainable innovation, technical excellence, and customer trust. We aspire to transform the way communities and industries use energy and technology by delivering solutions that are smarter, greener, and more cost-effective. By continually advancing our expertise and embracing emerging technologies, we aim to shape a future where sustainable engineering is the standard, contributing to Rwanda’s development and setting new benchmarks across Africa’s MEP industry.

Mission

At MSES LTD, our mission is to deliver high-quality, eco-efficient mechanical and electrical engineering solutions that meet the evolving needs of residential, commercial, and industrial clients. We are committed to innovation, sustainability, and service excellence, ensuring that every project we undertake enhances energy efficiency, safety, and long-term performance. Through the expertise of our dedicated team, we integrate green technologies, smart automation, and preventive maintenance strategies to provide solutions that are not only reliable today but also future-ready for tomorrow’s challenges. Guided by integrity and professionalism, we strive to be a trusted partner in driving sustainable development and technological advancement in Rwanda and beyond.
